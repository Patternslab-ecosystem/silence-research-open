# EU AI Act Compliance Timeline

**System:** PatternLens / SILENCE.OBJECTS
**Classification:** LIMITED RISK
**Key Deadline:** 2 August 2026

---

## Overview

The EU Artificial Intelligence Act entered into force on 1 August 2024. Obligations apply on a phased timeline based on system risk classification. For Limited Risk systems such as PatternLens, the primary enforcement date is **2 August 2026** — exactly two years after entry into force.

---

## Compliance Countdown

As of 2026-03-23, there are approximately **132 days** until the 2 August 2026 enforcement deadline.

All identified obligations are documented and either compliant or in active progress.
